About Us

At Science Sarathi, we are dedicated to making STEM education accessible, engaging, and hands-on for students, educators, and enthusiasts. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical application by offering innovative training programs in Robotics,Data Science,Web-development, Python, IoT, AI, ML, and Embedded Systems.
We specialize in:
✅ Industry-Oriented Training/Internships – Hands-on workshops and internships for school and college students.
✅ Workshops/Seminars for Educators/Students – Helping teachers/Students enhance their Studying methodologies with technology.
✅ STEM Curriculum Development – Aligned with NCERT, integrating real-world applications in Science & Mathematics.
✅ Career Guidance & ICT Training – Empowering students and teachers with future-ready skills.
